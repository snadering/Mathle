How to Play

Fill in the 4 operators between the numbers to make the equation true.

3 × 4 + 2 ÷ 5 − 1 = 11

You have 6 attempts. After each guess:

  • Green — correct operator, correct slot
  • Yellow — correct operator, wrong slot
  • Grey — this operator is not used

⚠️ Standard math rules apply — × and ÷ are evaluated before + and −.

Your guess must equal the result shown after the = sign, otherwise it won't be accepted.
